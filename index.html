08 Reflection

What I
Learned

Enterprise AI pushed me to design for expert users at scale — people who know exactly what they want but have zero patience for friction. Every interaction had to be fast, accurate, and recoverable. The AI interface especially required new design patterns around trust: users needed to see sources, understand confidence levels, and feel in control even when the AI was taking actions on their behalf.


The integration hub was the design challenge I underestimated most. 12+ third-party tools, each with different connection states, error modes, and usage patterns — unified into a single coherent panel without overwhelming the user. Hierarchical information architecture and progressive disclosure were the keys.

"Enterprise AI UX isn't about making AI look smart — it's about making humans feel in control of something incredibly powerful."
